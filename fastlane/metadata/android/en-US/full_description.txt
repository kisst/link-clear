Link Clear strips tracking parameters from URLs using the bundled ClearURLs
ruleset. It works entirely offline by default: paste or share a link, and get
back the same link with the tracking noise removed.

Three ways to clean a link:

* Share target — share a link to Link Clear from any app. You land on a
  confirmation screen showing the cleaned URL with Copy, Share, and Open
  actions. With "Automatic action" enabled, the chosen action runs immediately.
* Quick Settings tile — add the "Clean clipboard" tile to your Quick Settings
  panel. One tap cleans whatever is on the clipboard, in place.
* In-app editor — open the app for a Paste Clean / Paste Raw editor with a live
  before/after diff of exactly what was removed.

Privacy first:

* Offline by default. The cleaning ruleset is bundled, so no network is needed.
* No runtime permissions. Link Clear only acts when you explicitly share, tap
  the tile, or paste — it never watches your clipboard in the background.
* Two optional, off-by-default network features: resolving shortened links
  (t.co, bit.ly, and similar) before cleaning, and manually refreshing the
  bundled ruleset. Neither runs unless you turn it on.
